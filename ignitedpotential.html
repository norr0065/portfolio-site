Ignited Potential

Logo & Website Design

Ignited Potential is an up and coming executive coaching firm based in Ottawa. As an Executive Coach, Wayne Van Wyk wanted his logo to distinguish his brand and stand out as the leading executive coach in the Capital. While playing with the concept driven from igniting one's potential, this logo needed to exude confidence, power and relevance. The brand developed for Ignited Potential captures the fierce desire for success and the incentive to grow.
