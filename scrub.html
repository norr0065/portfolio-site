Scrub Inspired

Branding & Package Design

Scrub Inspired is a small Canadian business that originates from Ottawa and Cape Breton. Two young entrepreneurs started this handmade all-natural body scrub business in 2013, using only the finest natural ingredients. Scrub prides itself on natural, one-of-a-kind products and wanted to reflect that in their branding and design. The design inspiration is drawn from the bright colours, juicy aromas and raw natural ingridents found in each product.
