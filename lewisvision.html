Lewis Vision Improvement Foundation

Logo & Promotional Material

The Lewis Vision Improvement Foundation extend the values of an incredible company called eSight. eSight provides products and services to assist people who are affected by vision loss so that they to may live inclusive and normal lives. This organization is at the forefront vision impairment solution innovation. The LVIF brand inspires hope through the values of care and creativity to serve legally blind and low vision persons. The logo was created to represent the moments in life when sight is restored through the use of innovative vision improvement devices.
